About The Hackathon

The Geeta Internal Hackathon is an annual event that brings together the brightest minds from our university to solve real-world problems through technology.

This year's theme focuses on sustainable development goals, encouraging participants to develop solutions that can make a positive impact on society.

  • Open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Teams of 1-3 members
  • 24-hour coding marathon
  • Mentorship from industry experts
